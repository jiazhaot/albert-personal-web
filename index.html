About Me

I am a food scientist with a strong foundation in product development, ingredient functionality, and sensory evaluation. My academic path combined chemistry and nutrition, and my professional work has focused on translating research into reliable, scalable food experiences.

Photography has been my second lens on the world for years. I am drawn to everyday rituals, quiet urban corners, and the geometry of natural landscapes. My camera practice sharpens my observation and complements my scientific work by keeping me attentive to detail, texture, and nuance.

I believe good work blends rigor with curiosity. Whether I am optimizing a formula or documenting a morning walk, I aim to create things that are clear, honest, and useful. This site is a small archive of the paths I am exploring and the stories I am collecting along the way.
